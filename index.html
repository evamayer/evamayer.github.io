The Murder of a Data Analyst

An SQL Murder Mystery

This SQL murder mystery is designed to be both a tool to practice SQL concepts and a fun game to solve a fictional murder case.

The Brief

There’s been a murder!
A crime has taken place and Detective Taggart needs your help. He advised you that the crime was a murder that occurred in the evening hours on January 6, 2023. It took place in the Baltimore office of Hyper Incorporated. Start your investigation by retrieving the corresponding crime scene report from the police department’s database. After that you may also want to take a closer look at the interactive dashboard below.
